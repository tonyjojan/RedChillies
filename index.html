About

Nestled in the Silicon Valley suburbs of Milpitas is our Malabar kitchen, Red Chillies. It was created by a food loving crew on a passion to share their family recipes with our clienteles to have a feel of ‘kitchen away from home’ while dining. Along with the great family recipes offered at Red Chilies, is the at home feeling you get the moment you walk in. You will be greeted with a warm welcome and instantly feel as if you are in a quaint Malabar villa. The warm welcome you received at the door will be continued throughout your stay because of our dedicated hospitality. We want to encourage you to come, have a meal with us and experience our Malabar kitchen.
